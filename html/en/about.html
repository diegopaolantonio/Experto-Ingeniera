About Experto Ingenieria

EXPERTO INGENIERIA specializes in industrial automation creating, correcting and improving industrial processes, mainly focused on programming, electrical design and electrical installations, leading my clients to have more efficient and better quality processes. Having worked in factories of multiple items such as automotive, paper, rubber, glass, among others; and always respecting the brands chosen by clients, some of them being SIEMENS, ALLEN BRADLEY, SCHNEIDER, DELTA, etc.

Objetives

The main objective of EXPERTO INGENIERIA is to improve the situation of many industries that need to improve their production systems, through the implementation of high quality devices, designing, modifying and adding more modern and better quality automation, which helps to be more competitive. , always respecting the work ethic, leading us to both professional and personal growth in each work.

History

I started in 2013 making automatisms with our own electronic circuits, due to the increase in the demand for work, I migrated to automation systems with PLC, since it is easier to obtain commercial responses, which led me to larger clients, but without stopping serving those who trusted me first, who still need a reliable provider to give them an efficient solution.
