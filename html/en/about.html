About Experto Ingenieria

EXPERTO INGENIERIA specializes in industrial automation creating, correcting and improving industrial processes, mainly focused on programming, electrical design and electrical installations, leading my clients to have more efficient and better quality processes. Having worked in factories of multiple items such as automotive, paper, rubber, glass, among others; and always respecting the brands chosen by clients, some of them being SIEMENS, ALLEN BRADLEY, SCHNEIDER, DELTA, etc.

Objetives

The main objective of EXPERTO INGENIERIA is to improve the situation of many industries that need to improve their production systems, designing and modifying automatisms, for more modern technology and better quality, which helps to be more competitive, always respecting the work ethic, leading us to both professional and personal growth in each work.

History

I started in 2013 designing automatisms with electronic circuits of my own design for a company, due to the demand for work, I migrated to automation systems with PLC, since automatisms can be made with more generic hardware, and quick replacement, although it increases the costs of the works, which led me to larger clients, but without stopping serving those who trusted me first, and who still need a reliable supplier that gives them efficient solutions.
